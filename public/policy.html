Privacy Policy

A TO Z Science Centre

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to A TO Z Science Centre. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name provided during quizzes or registration.
  • Email address (if you contact or register with us).
  • Quiz scores and learning progress.
  • Browser and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve your learning experience.
  • To provide quiz results and progress tracking.
  • To improve website performance.
  • To respond to your questions or feedback.

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and remember preferences. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as YouTube for educational videos. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our educational content is intended for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent where required.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Contact page of our website.
